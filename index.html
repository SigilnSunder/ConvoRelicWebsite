Why ConvoRelic?

Why not?!
We gather mementos throughout our life, like photos, videos, letters, and voice recordings. So when our founder's mother listens to her late husband's old voicemails about hiding the bread, we thought we can give that unique loving memory a physical form. Looking at a beautiful reminder of a hilarious time should be possible for everyone. So here we are.
